About Us

Want to serve with fun? Want to make friends around the globe? Want to bring a smile on someone's face? Become a member

R.O.N.M.I.A




RONMIA brings together students and working professionals to exchange thoughts with other rotaractors, to help a person in developing his/her personality, developing skills, having bonding activities through service and creating future leaders in this process. RONMIA was chartered in 2013, charter president being Rtr.Roopal. After that, the club has only grown leaps and bounds and given some very prominent and talented leaders to RID3142. Currently, RONMIA is a platinum club and each year, the club churns out new star rotaractors, who, bring to table, new ideas and concepts which are implemented into projects which work for betterment of community and individuals. Not only this,every year,through club bonding activities, many new friendships are created in RONMIA which often turn out to be lifelong friendships. With so many achievements, RONMIA stands tall and strong as a club with currently more than 53 members.

District 3142




R.I.D. 3142, initiated in the year 2015-2016, has gloriously evolved, comprising of 57 clubs with more than 3500 Rotaractors as a part of this movement. Ranging from Navi Mumbai to Badlapur, R.I.D. 3142 is divided into four zones as mentioned- Zone 1- Navi Mumbai, Vashi, Nerul, Kharghar. Zone 2- Thane and Bhiwandi. Zone 3- Dombivli and Kalyan. Zone 4– Ulhasnagar, Ambernath and Badlapur. Each zone has approximately 12-15 clubs, led by the Zonal Rotaract Representative (Z.R.R.) and each club in the zone has a President to take it ahead. All the officials of the Club Click here to know More, Zone, D.R.R and his Council work for one Rotaract year, (i.e. 1st July to 30th June) We belong to Zone 1 and are proud Rotaractors of RID3142!

Club Goals




R.O.N.M.I.A. has the following goals: ● To develop professional and leadership skills ● To emphasize respect for the rights of others, based on recognition of the worth of each individual ● To recognize the dignity and value of all useful occupations as opportunities to serve ● To recognize, practice, and promote ethical standards as leadership qualities and vocational responsibilities ● To develop knowledge and understanding of the needs, problems, and opportunities in the community and worldwide ● To provide opportunities for personal and group activities to serve the community and promote international understanding and goodwill toward all people.
